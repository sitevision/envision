Colors

Color classes and variables for elements, components and layouts.

  • Basic colors

    The “Basic colors” section defines general colors for backgrounds, text, links, and borders.

  • Block colors

    The “Block colors” section offers preset color combinations for large content areas.

  • Brand colors

    The “Brand Colors” section defines primary brand hues with contrast-friendly shades for accessibility.

  • Element colors

    The “Element Colors” section defines specific shades for buttons, icons, and interactive elements.

  • Status colors

    The “Status Colors” section provides distinct hues representing success, warnings, errors, and informational states.
